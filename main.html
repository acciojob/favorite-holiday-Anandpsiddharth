Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India, famous for its beaches, nightlife, vibrant culture, and scenic beauty. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Known for water sports and lively atmosphere.
  2. Calangute Beach – One of the most popular and crowded beaches.
  3. Fort Aguada – A historical Portuguese fort with sea views.
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – A stunning four-tiered waterfall.
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A famous UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Chicken Xacuti
  • Bebinca (Goan Cake)
  • Feni (Local Drink)
